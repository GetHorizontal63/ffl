Use this form to request an injury substitution during a game. This will replace an injured player with a bench player.

The game must be in progress or have ended within the last 30 minutes.

Injury Substitution Rules

  • Substitutions are only allowed if a player gets injured during a game.
  • The injured player must be in your starting lineup.
  • The substitute player must be on your bench.
  • Substitution requests must be submitted during the game or within 30 minutes after it ends.
  • Substitutions are processed immediately and are not reversible.
  • Commissioners may review and approve/deny all substitution requests.
